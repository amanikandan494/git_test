Maggi Masala Noodles

Description

Maggi masala noodles is a favourite go to snack for the people of Indian subcontinent as it is easy to cook and can be made within a couple of minutes.

Ingredients

  • Masala Maggi Noodles(4 cakes)
  • Onions(2Nos)
  • Green Chilly(3-4 Nos)
  • Oil(1 table spoon)
  • Water(2 cups)
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. On the stove and pour 2 tablespoons of Oil in the pan and heat the oil for a while.
  2. After the oil is heated well
  3. Put two to three onions along with two to three green chillies
  4. Stir the mixture well and wait until the onions change colour.
  5. Add the taste maker provided along with the maggi package to the mixture and stir well.
  6. Add water to the mixture and wait until it begins to boil
  7. Add four cakes of the maggi masala noodles and cook for 3 to 4 minutes until the noodles are cooked well
  8. Serve the dish hot
